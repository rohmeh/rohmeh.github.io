Rohan Mehra
MS Student, INP-ISIMA

I'm a Computer Science Master’s Student at the INP-ISIMA, Université Clermont Auvergne. Currently, I am advised by Prof. Gilles Venturini at LIFAT - Université de Tours, where my research focuses on explainable AI approaches for bee image classification. Prior to this, I worked as part of the Institut Pascal lab, advised by Dr. Mathieu Labussière where where my project focused on short-wave infrared (SWIR) camera's adaptation to autonomous driving application.

I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal, India in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I conducted my BS project under the supervison of Dr. Arpit Sharma, focusing on using zero-shot learning approaches for requirement classification.
